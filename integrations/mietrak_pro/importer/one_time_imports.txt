GeneralLedgerAccount query:

Select AccountNumber, GeneralLedgerAccount.Description FROM GeneralLedgerAccount
INNER JOIN GeneralLedgerAccountType
ON GeneralLedgerAccountTypeFK = GeneralLedgerAccountTypePK
WHERE (NotVisible = 0 OR NotVisible IS NULL) AND GeneralLedgerAccountType.Code NOT IN ('001', '002', '006')
ORDER BY AccountNumber
